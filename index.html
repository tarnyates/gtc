Golden Trout Camp

Rustic High Sierra Camp in the Golden Trout Wilderness

Fist developed in 1903 as a back country store, this high sierra camp is set adjacent too cottonwood creek, is surrounded by meadows, and has a spectacular view of Mt. Langley
